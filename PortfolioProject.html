IDEATION

HOW IT
STARTED

Before joining this diploma, i wanted to be a fashion designer , but due to academic reasons could not fulfil my dreams. I enjoyed doing design due o the fact of being able to be creative and free through a form of art.

WHY DID I MAKE THIS

I did this because i noticed that numerous shopping websites either have the same layout and navigation which made me motivated to try to add my unique twist . I took inspirations from Art gallery magazines and how they displayed their featured arts and the inspiration. I feel that fashion websites should encourage people to show expression in the clothes they wear. Hence the name CONCEPT, as i wanted this shopping website but a place where people can truely take inspiration from us and make a change in their wardrobe to express their emotions through what they wear.

EXTRA INFORMATION

This website was inspired by famous streetwear brands as well as home grown singapore streetwear start-ups mixed with the different cultures and influences.

DESIGN

I then decided to start on making a lofi wireframe as well as a hifi wireframe
using adobe XD to visualise my ideas on a rough layout and placement of
various UI elements around the page

This is an image of my LOFI wireframe

This is an image of my HIFI wireframe

A wireframe is an important part of designing ,without it i will be unable to visualise my ideas. i decided to go with a horizontal navigation at the top as well as a 3-grid 1fr layout for the home page. I used different elements and principles of design and implemented them into my design.

PROTOTYPING

This was the time to add the effects and connect the pages together using the prototyping function. It was my first time implementing such a fucntion using adobe XD.
I played around with various plug-in and hover effects to make my UI design look unique and fresh for the user.

The process of prototyping connsisted of alot of research and tutorials which have aided me in finishing up the linking for the various pages present in the XD document
